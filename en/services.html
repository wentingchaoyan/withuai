Development Support Platform

WithU is a platform designed to provide individualized support for children with disabilities. We combine AI-powered analysis with expert knowledge to offer personalized developmental support tailored to each child's unique characteristics.

  • Visualization of Individual Developmental Characteristics
  • Stage-Appropriate Record Management
  • Individual Support Goal Setting
  • Collaboration with Expert Teams

AI-Powered Development Analysis

Our AI analyzes children's daily movements and behaviors to objectively evaluate developmental characteristics and challenges. Based on professional analysis, we propose individualized support methods.

  • Detailed Movement and Behavior Analysis
  • Objective Evaluation of Developmental Characteristics
  • Individual Growth Reports
  • Expert Verification of Analysis Results

Growth Record Sharing

Securely share developmental records among family members, therapy centers, schools, and medical institutions. The entire team works together to support the child's growth.

  • Secure Information Sharing
  • Multi-Disciplinary Team Collaboration
  • Centralized Development Records
  • Role-Based Access Control

Individual Support Reports

We provide detailed analysis of each child's development and propose individualized support plans. Regular progress evaluations and necessary adjustments to support methods are conducted.

  • Detailed Development Analysis
  • Individual Support Plan Proposals
  • Regular Progress Evaluations
  • Support Method Optimization
